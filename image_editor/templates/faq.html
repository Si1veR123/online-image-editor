  • Can I close the window and return to editing later?

    Yes, the website uses a cookie to identify your device. Images are deleted on our server after 2 weeks or 1 week of inactivity. Images are also cleared every month.

  • How do I use the editor?

    Upload your image here. If you have already uploaded an image, press the close button on the editor, and try again. You can then use the crop, rotate and adjust tools to edit your image. Press the help button for information on how to use the tools.

  • Why do I need JavaScript enabled?

    JavaScript allows the website to format itself correctly, and contact the server to edit your images.
